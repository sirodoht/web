Work Philosophy

  1. I work with speed in mind (but never at the expense of quality or security). In the last 10 years I have joined more than 15 separate teams of software and infrastructure engineers. This background has equiped me with skills that enable me to fairly quickly become familiar with new codebases and architectures. Upon joining a team, my primary objective is to learn how to collaborate and communicate with the team effectively, with the goal of submitting a PR by the second day.
  2. I join teams gradually to facilitate minimal risk for the hiring manager (and myself). I start with 2 days per week, and grow to 5 days after 1 or 2 months.
  3. I post a report (usually in the form of a written standup) for every day that I work, outlining the content of my work. I do not accept payment unless there is complete satisfaction with my work from the stakeholders. This is achieved through clear, structured, and relevant communication between me, the team, the stakeholders.
  4. My daily rate is $1,000. I provide invoices at the end of each month, with payment terms requiring settlement within 30 days.
