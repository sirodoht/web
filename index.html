Hi, I’m Theodore.

Most of the time, I'm trying to understand how we can make useful software. I think it needs to be fast and simple. I publish some of this work on GitHub.

Projects:

Mataroa is an open source blogging platform based on the design philosophy of minimalism with strong data interoperability implementation rules.

Laniakea Books is a public domain publishing house based out of London, England. We publish brand new books which are dedicated to the public domain from day one. Read about our philosophy here.

Read my book, Letters from Prison, a collection of ideas on society and its prisonhood.

Chaitin School is an in-person group of people interested in software engineering as a fun activity of leisure time as well as a craft to become skilled at. Our values are inclusivity, expertise, humour, gifting, democracy, exploration, community. Join us! We are in London, England.

Newspeak House is an independent unaccredited residential college founded in 2015 in which I contribute as member and lecturer. We have a course on political technology.

I keep a blog at nutcroft.com. See the table of contents.

Everything I write is dedicated to the public domain.
