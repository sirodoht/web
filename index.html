Projects:

Mataroa is an open source blogging platform based on the design philosophy of minimalism with strong data interoperability implementation rules.

Laniakea Books is a public domain publishing house based out of London, England. We publish brand new books which are dedicated to the public domain from day one. Read about our philosophy here.

Letters from Prison, published by Laniakea Books, is a collection of ideas on society and its prisonhood.

London College of Political Technology, Newspeak House is an independent residential college founded in 2015 in which I contribute as member and lecturer. We have a course on political technology. I teach a module on open source.

Shoshin College is an experimental learning co-operative. We are interested in offering low-cost yet high-quality classes on a variety of disciplines in London, England.
